# Shapes 3D

3D geometric shapes with raymarching.

## Parameters

| Parameter | Type | Default | Range | Description |
|-----------|------|---------|-------|-------------|
| seed | int | 1 | 1–100 | Random seed |
| shapeA | int | 30 | capsuleHoriz/capsuleVert/cube/cylinderHoriz/cylinderVert/octahedron/sphere/torusHoriz/torusVert | Primary shape |
| shapeB | int | 10 | capsuleHoriz/capsuleVert/cube/cylinderHoriz/cylinderVert/octahedron/sphere/torusHoriz/torusVert | Secondary shape |
| shapeAScale | float | — | — | Scale of shape A |
| shapeBScale | float | — | — | Scale of shape B |
| shapeAThickness | float | — | — | Thickness of shape A |
| shapeBThickness | float | — | — | Thickness of shape B |
| blendMode | int | — | — | Shape blend mode |
| smoothness | float | — | — | Blend smoothness |
| spin | float | — | — | Spin rotation |
| flip | float | — | — | Flip rotation |
| spinSpeed | float | — | — | Spin animation speed |
| flipSpeed | float | — | — | Flip animation speed |
| repetition | float | — | — | Shape repetition |
| cameraDist | float | — | — | Camera distance |
| backgroundOpacity | float | — | — | Background opacity |
