## Parameters

- **Kernel Id**: Selects the convolution kernel (800=Blur, 801=Deriv X, 802=Deriv Y, 803=Edges, 804=Emboss, 805=Invert, 806=Random, 807=Sharpen, 808=Sobel X, 809=Sobel Y, 810=Box Blur).
- **Normalize**: Re-normalize the result after filtering.
- **Alpha**: Blend between the original image and the filtered result.
