## Arguments

### `channel`
- **Type:** `channel` enum.
- **Default:** `channel.r`.
- **Values:** `channel.r`, `channel.g`, `channel.b`, `channel.a`.
- **Description:** Which channel to extract.

### `scale`
- **Type:** Number.
- **Default:** `1`.
- **Range:** -10–10.
- **Description:** Overall scale of the effect.

### `offset`
- **Type:** Number.
- **Default:** `0`.
- **Range:** -10–10.
- **Description:** Offset amount applied to the effect.
